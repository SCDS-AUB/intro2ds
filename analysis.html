Methodology

This analysis was generated by:

  1. Extracting 15,982 articles from AUB ScholarWorks (English, all years)
  2. Filtering to 4,091 recent articles (2022-2026)
  3. Matching articles to DS modules via keyword analysis
  4. Aggregating authors by department affiliation

Limitations: Authors without department info may be external collaborators. Some AUB faculty may be underrepresented if their recent work isn't in ScholarWorks.
