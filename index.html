About

This site hosts lecture materials for the School of Data Science and Computing (SCDS) at the American University of Beirut. Our courses combine computational thinking with real-world data from diverse disciplines.

"Data science is the practice of building stable action-perception cycles with what is given."

Content Pipeline

Each lecture follows this workflow:

  1. Transcript - Voice notes or written outline
  2. Notebook - Interactive Jupyter notebook with code
  3. Markdown - Web-ready formatted content
  4. Website - Published here for students
